# Free Business Automation Workflow Templates

Supercharge your business operations with our collection of free, ready-to-use automation workflow templates. Whether you're looking to streamline sales, enhance customer support, or manage leads more effectively, these templates are designed to help you save time and improve efficiency.

## Why Use These Templates?

*   **Free to Use:** Get started with automation without any cost.
*   **Easy to Implement:** Designed for quick setup and integration with your existing systems.
*   **Boost Efficiency:** Automate repetitive tasks and free up your team to focus on what matters most.
*   **Scalable:** Adapt and customize the templates to fit your specific business needs.

## Available Workflow Categories

This repository offers a variety of templates across different business functions:

*   **[Sales](./sales/)**: Automate your sales pipeline, from lead to close.
*   **[Customer Support](./customer-support/)**: Enhance your customer service with automated ticketing and support.
*   **[Lead Management](./lead-management/)**: Streamline lead capture, nurturing, and qualification.
*   **[IT Ops](./it-ops/)**: Simplify your IT operations with automated infrastructure and incident management.
*   **[Marketing](./marketing/)**: Power up your marketing campaigns with automation.

## Getting Started

1.  Browse the directories to find a workflow that suits your needs.
2.  Follow the instructions in the `README.md` file within each directory to implement the template.
3.  Customize the workflow to match your specific requirements.
